[ About me ]

Faisal Badrom, born in 1981 in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia has set his passion on “Information Technology (IT)” since year 2003. Holding a Diploma in Computer Science and Information Technology with more than 19 years of working experience in the IT Industry; Mr Faisal was fortunate to work and involve in several high profile IT companies and projects. Married to an chartered accountant with 3 children, he believes in seventy percent of your technical knowledge enhancements is from your own effort and intuitive with ten percent is gained from traditional method of learning and mentoring. Faisal Badrom is currently the Group Technical Director for GFIS (M) Sdn Bhd.

During his tenure as an IT Engineer, he also holds a Certified ORACLE Professional Majoring in Oracle Database and Oracle Golden Gate Certified Implementation Specialist. To add to his string of certifications, Faisal is also a Certified Hewlett Packard Engineer with several specialisation namely; HP Accredited Integration Specialist: StorageWorks; HP Accredited Integration Specialist: Data Protector and HP-UX Certified System Administrator. To add to Faisal’s achievements, he was also the main architect and implementer to implement the single biggest Emergency Response Database System together with its Security Layer Nationwide in Malaysia in the year 2010, which its implementation and configuration became a reference internationally to one the biggest Database provider at that time. The database and the Emergency Response System is still being maintained and used until now.

Being an IT Infrastructure Technologist, Faisal believe in the saying " Solution Is Within Your Reach When You Are Open To Being Creative; and Being Creative One Must Leave their Comfort Zone”. This holds the belief that solid infrastructure plays a major role in determining the success of any system, ensuring any IT system should have MAA (Maximum Availability Architecture) embedded within the design.

Entering the new phase in his career in end of year 2021, Faisal took on the next challenge – as a technologist and as an entrepreneur; with the formation of GFIS Innovative Solutions Sdn Bhd incorporate in November 2021 under its parent company GFIS (M) Sdn Bhd. As one of its Shareholders and Directors, Faisal is enthusiastic and confident with all the skills, knowledge, experience and determination he is able to Innovate further his passion at the same time making the opportunity to develop young local talents to use their creativeness and skills to embrace combination of modern tech-stack to become a solution hence following the current key trends of open platform and technology from Application Definition and Development, Orchestration & Management, Provisioning, Platform and also Observability and Analysis.

Faisal plays a vital role in realising the vision GFIS (M) Sdn Bhd’s innovative technology ventures to be the "Center of Tech-Stack in Asia" which enable the opportunities for collaboration joint venture with local and global partners in designing, developing and deployment of systems and prototyping with MVP (Minimum Viable Product). He is optimistic in achieving this at minimum within two years ahead.
